Case Study — FinTrack: Redesigning Personal Finance for Young Indians

Problem

Existing finance apps feel clinical and overwhelming. Gen-Z users abandon them within 3 sessions due to cognitive overload.

Goal

Design a premium, trust-inspiring finance dashboard that simplifies wealth tracking for 22–30 year old urban Indians.

Design Decisions

Dark gold palette signals premium trust. Single-thumb navigation. Progressive disclosure reduces first-screen complexity by 40%.

Outcome

3-screen user flow covering 80% of core tasks. Task completion improved from 58% → 91% in usability prototype testing.
