Case Study — MindSpace: Designing Emotional Safety in a Wellness App

Problem

1 in 3 young Indians reports daily anxiety, yet existing wellness apps feel foreign, clinical, or gamified in ways that cheapen the experience.

Approach

Led discovery interviews with 12 users aged 20–28. Identified key need: emotional safety before feature discovery. Soft onboarding, no pressure.

Design System

Cosmic dark palette + italic serif typography creates intimacy. Breathing animations use 4s cycles matching real physiological calm response.

PM Metrics

Target KPIs: D7 retention 55%+, avg. session 8 min, journal completion 3x/week. Streak mechanic designed to reward consistency, not frequency.
