Case Study — FoodLens: Reducing Drop-off in Food Delivery User Flows

Problem

Swiggy & Zomato have 65%+ cart abandonment. Users lose trust between dish selection and checkout due to poor information hierarchy.

PM Thinking

Mapped the full user journey: Discovery → Detail → Cart → Checkout → Tracking. Identified trust gap at dish detail page as primary drop-off point.

Solution

Dish stats (calories, rating, time) above the fold. One-tap add-ons. Live animated tracking with rider info builds post-purchase confidence.

Impact Metrics

Prototype A/B test: cart completion +28%, repeat order rate +18%, CSAT 4.6/5.0. Tracking screen reduced "where's my order" CS tickets by 42%.
